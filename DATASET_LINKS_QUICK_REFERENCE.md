# Security Dataset Links - Quick Reference
## Direct Links for Account & Entity Compromise Datasets

---

## 🎯 Account Compromise Datasets

### **1. CERT Insider Threat Dataset ⭐ BEST**
**Link:** https://www.cert.org/insider-threat-tools/datasets/

**Why:** Most comprehensive UEBA dataset with real-world account compromise scenarios

**Access:** Free registration required

---

### **2. GoMask Account Takeover Detection**
**Link:** https://gomask.ai/marketplace/datasets/account-takeover-detection

**Why:** Synthetic dataset specifically for account takeover detection

**Access:** May require registration

---

### **3. Have I Been Pwned (HIBP) API**
**Link:** https://haveibeenpwned.com/API/v3

**Why:** Real-time API to check compromised accounts

**Access:** Free API key available

**API Docs:** https://haveibeenpwned.com/API/v3

---

### **4. Pwned Passwords Dataset**
**Link:** https://github.com/imavantikahere/Pwned-Password-Dataset

**Alternative:** https://haveibeenpwned.com/Passwords

**Why:** 600M+ compromised passwords for analysis

**Access:** Direct download

---

## 🎯 Entity Compromise Datasets

### **1. CERT Insider Threat Dataset (Same as Above)**
**Link:** https://www.cert.org/insider-threat-tools/datasets/

**Why:** Includes entity-level activities (devices, IPs, resources)

---

### **2. Entity Resolution Benchmark**
**Link:** https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/4whpm32y47/1

**Why:** Entity behavior patterns and resolution workflows

**Access:** Direct download

---
