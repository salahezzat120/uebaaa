# S-UEBA Responsibility Matrix (RACI)
## Who Does What - Quick Reference

**Legend:**
- **R** = Responsible (does the work)
- **A** = Accountable (approves/owns the outcome)
- **C** = Consulted (provides input)
- **I** = Informed (kept in the loop)

## Escalation Path

2. **Timeline Issue:** Member → Team Leader → Advisor
3. **Resource Issue:** Team Leader → Advisor
